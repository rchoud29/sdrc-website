About Us

Sun Devil Robotics Club hosts a national level robotics team competing in the Mar's Rover Society's University Rover Challange and leads the way for the next generation of engineers in a beginner friendly internal rover competition.

Our mission is to provide our members with hands- on experience in robotics, cultivate problem solving skills, participating in competitions, and inspire them to pursure careers in STEM fields.
