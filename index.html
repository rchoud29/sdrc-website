About Us

Sun Devil Robotics Club is committed to pushing forward robotics at every level. Our URC team competes on the international stage in the Mars Rover Society's University Rover Challenge. Racing Rovers makes robotics accessible, teaching students everything they need to build their own autonomous rover and compete with other rovers in an internal competition.
Our mission is to provide our members with hands-on experience in robotics, cultivate problem solving skills, participating in competitions, and inspire them to pursure careers in STEM fields.
